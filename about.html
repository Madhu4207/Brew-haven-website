About Brew Haven

The story behind the smell of fresh coffee every morning

Our Story

Brew Haven started in 2020 as a tiny cart outside our college campus, run by two friends, Arjun and Divya, who couldn't survive without their daily filter coffee. What began as a small experiment turned into a full cafe that our neighbourhood now calls "the coffee place."

We still roast our beans in small batches and every recipe on our menu has been tested (and re-tested) by our own team before it made it to your cup.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  1. Serve honest, quality coffee at a fair price.
  2. Support local farmers by sourcing beans directly.
  3. Create a space where students and families feel at home.

Our Vision

To become the neighbourhood's favourite coffee corner - one cup, one conversation at a time.
